Tips to take care of plants in Bangalore

1. Choose appropriate plants depending on the light and ventilation of your home or office setting. This will ensure that your plant stays healthy.
2. Regular Watering- It is important to water your plants at frequent intervals to prevent them from drying out and dying. Try watering your plants as and when the top two inches of the soil are dry to touch.
3. Pruning- Trim and dead and dry branches and remove dried off leaves to ensure that the plant's healthy parts get more nutrition and aid to its growth.
